MY STORY

From Lab Experiments to Product Experiments

My journey began in a chemistry lab, where I built a foundation in scientific methods and analytical thinking. With dual Masters degrees in Chemistry and Environmental Science, I entered the professional world equipped with a unique lens for problem-solving.

At the City of San Diego, I discovered a critical gap: brilliant environmental solutions lacking intuitive software interfaces. I found myself naturally translating between technical experts and end-users, which sparked my transition into product management.

"The most valuable product managers are those who can bridge technical complexity with user simplicity. My science background gives me a unique advantage in building sustainability products that actually work."
My Product Philosophy

Today, I blend my technical expertise with product leadership at UL Solutions, where I've driven 15% revenue growth through user-centered approaches and data-driven decision making. The scientific method I learned years ago now guides how I test and validate product hypotheses in the high-tech sustainability space.
